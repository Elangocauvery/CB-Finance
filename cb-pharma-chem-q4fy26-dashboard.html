Sudeep Pharma — 3 Verticals
  • Pharma & Food Grade Minerals — calcium, magnesium, potassium, iron, zinc, sodium, copper salts used as excipients/actives across pharma, food and industrial applications. Core legacy business.
  • Specialty Ingredients — AbsorBis branded high-absorption nutraceutical ingredients (sleep, muscle recovery); new battery-grade materials line, Phase 1 commissioning targeted April 2027. Grew from ₹172 Cr to ₹280 Cr in FY26 (now 44% of revenue).
  • Nutrition (NSS Europe) — 85%-owned Nutrition Supplies Services (Netherlands), vitamin/mineral premix blends, acquired FY26 for a European manufacturing + customer-approval foothold.
Beta Drugs — 3 Verticals
  • Oncology Formulations (Own Brand) — tablets, capsules, injections, lyophilized injections for cancer treatment; top-10 Indian oncology player; 6 brands tracking toward ₹5 Cr+ each in FY26.
  • API / CDMO — backward-integrated oncology API manufacturing (Adley Labs) plus contract manufacturing for ~30 domestic/global CDMO clients.
  • Dermatology / Cosmeceuticals — newly profitable division; exclusive in-licensing with Italian major Promoitalia for derma fillers/mesotherapy, targeting ₹30-100 Cr sales over 3-5 years.
Shilpa Medicare — 4 Verticals
  • API — Onco and Non-Onco complex APIs; ₹985 Cr FY26 revenue (+16% YoY); 37 new DMF filings during the year.
  • Formulations — complex injectables and NCEs; ₹618 Cr FY26 revenue (+30% YoY); pipeline includes Abraxane and Enzalutamide tablets (FY28), Rotigotine Transdermal Patch (Europe, FY27).
  • Biologics — ~₹150 Cr FY26 revenue (~+100% YoY); Aflibercept and Nivolumab biosimilars in human clinical studies, approvals expected FY27.
  • ADC (Antibody-Drug Conjugates) — emerging integrated manufacturing capability; facility qualification and Teduglutide lab development advancing.
Privi Speciality Chemicals — Single Segment, Multiple Product Lines
  • Core Aroma Chemicals — musks, ambroxides and other key fragrance/flavour intermediates supplied globally to F&F houses; reported as one segment under Ind AS 108.
  • Givaudan JV (PRIGIV) — joint venture for high-end specialty aroma products; turned profitable for the first time in Q4 FY26.
  • New Product Pipeline — ethyl maltol, maltol, cyclopentanone (mechanical completion targeted June 2027); biomass-conversion demonstration plant (₹70-75 Cr) in basic engineering stage.
  • Capacity Expansion — Phase 1 (+54,000 MTPA) due 30 Jun 2026, underpinning the "5k:1k" medium-term vision for FY29-30.
