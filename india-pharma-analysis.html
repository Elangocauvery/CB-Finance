CDMO = Contract Development & Manufacturing Organisation. These companies manufacture drugs/APIs on behalf of global innovators under contract — highly differentiated, sticky revenues, IP-light model. India is gaining share from China due to geopolitical supply chain shifts ("China+1").

📊 CDMO Opportunity Sizing
Global CDMO market: ~$250 Bn by 2030 (CAGR 8%+)
India share: ~4-5% currently, targeting 10%+ by 2030
China+1 beneficiaries: Sai Life, Laurus, Divi's, Neuland
Sai Life FY26 growth: ~35% revenue CAGR (FY23-FY26)
Laurus CDMO FY26: ₹2,080 Cr (+36% YoY)
Divi's Custom Synthesis: ~₹1,500-1,800 Cr annually

Why CDMO commands premium valuation:
• Long-term contracts (5-10 yr) = revenue visibility
• IP protected by client NDA; sticky relationships
• Capacity utilisation leverage to margins
• Lower regulatory risk vs finished dose generics
