Business Model Analysis

Revenue engines, USPs, and structural advantages

Titan — The Conglomerate Jewellery Model

Multi-Brand Architecture

  • Tanishq — flagship mass-premium, 22K/18K mix
  • Mia by Tanishq — lightweight workwear, 14K/18K
  • Zoya — ultra-luxury, diamonds, HNI
  • CaratLane — online-first, daily wear (61% stake)
  • Tanishq International — USA, UAE, UK, Singapore

Golden Harvest Scheme (GHS)

  • ~7–8 million active subscribers (installment savings)
  • ₹3,600 Cr deferred liability = captive demand float
  • Customers locked for 10–11 months before purchase

Manufacturing Moat

  • Hosur factory — India's largest vertically integrated jewellery plant
  • 200+ in-house designers | 1,500+ new designs per quarter

Watches & Wearables

  • Titan, Sonata, Fastrack, Helios — multi-price portfolio
  • Smartwatches: Titan Smart, Fastrack Reflex
  • Licensed brands: Tommy Hilfiger, Hugo Boss

Titan Eye+ — Eyewear (~940 stores)

  • Bundled optometry = recurring visits, high loyalty
  • Sunwear + prescription + contacts = full wallet share

COCO + FOCO Franchise

  • COCO — metro flagships (company-owned/operated)
  • FOCO — Tier 2/3 (franchisee capex, Titan ops)
  • ~60% of new FY26 stores via FOCO = asset-light scaling

Kalyan — The Franchise Scale Model

FOCO Hyper-Expansion

  • Franchisee funds capex; Kalyan owns inventory + ops
  • ~55% of India showrooms franchise-owned
  • Target: 800+ showrooms by FY27 (from 542 now)
  • New store payback: 18–24 months

My Kalyan Sub-Network

  • 1,800+ collection centres in Tier 3/4 towns
  • 4M+ installment scheme subscribers

Candere — Digital Arm

  • Online-first platform, reached profitability FY25
  • ~4% of India revenues

Middle East (USD Revenue)

  • 62 showrooms: UAE, Oman, Kuwait, Qatar, Bahrain
  • Indian diaspora + Gulf locals — 22K gold demand
  • FY26 ME revenue: ~₹4,200 Cr (~14% of total)

Regional Brand Trust

  • Founded Kerala 1993 — South India stronghold
  • BIS hallmarking transparency = organised shift beneficiary

USA Entry FY26

  • 2 showrooms — New Jersey (Indian diaspora focus)
  • Early stage, long-term option value

Buyer Analysis & Changing Trends

Customer segments, evolving preferences, and demand shifts

Revenue by Customer Segment

Purchase Occasion Mix

8 Key Buyer Shifts

1. Studded/Diamond Preference Rising
22K plain → 18K studded. Titan studded mix: 35% (FY26) vs 27% (FY22). Higher margin, driven by younger buyers and gifting culture.
2. Lightweight Daily-Wear Boom
Working women driving office-wear gold. Mia, CaratLane boom. Daily wear now 22% of Titan revenues in FY26.
3. Online Research → Offline Purchase
73% of buyers research online first. CaratLane try-at-home + in-store pickup = winning model.
4. Millennials Entering Bridal Market
1990–98 birth cohort at peak wedding age. Larger budgets, design-opinionated buyers.
5. Lab-Grown Diamond Watch
LGD prices down 70% in 4 years. Risk to studded margin over FY27–29.
6. BIS Hallmarking → Organised Shift
Mandatory hallmarking since 2021. Organised share: 12% (FY20) → 18% (FY26). Direct benefit to Titan, Kalyan.
7. Gold as Investment
Gold coins/bars up 45% in FY26. High prices trigger exchange/upgrade behaviour.
8. NRI Diaspora Surge
Indian gold 25–30% cheaper than buying abroad. Kalyan Gulf + Tanishq USA capturing this.

Gold Metal Loan (GML) — Deep Dive

How GML Works

  • Borrow physical gold from banks — not cash
  • Interest rate: 2.5–4.5% p.a. vs 8–10% for cash loans
  • Manufactured into jewellery, repaid at maturity (30–180 days)
  • No gold price exposure on borrowed quantity

Structural Moat

  • Small jewellers cannot access GML (no creditworthiness)
  • Titan GML: ₹5,200 Cr at ~3% — saving ~₹285 Cr/yr vs cash
  • Permanent cost advantage vs unorganised sector

GML Risks

  • Gold price rally → repayment in gold more expensive
  • RBI can restrict GML limits or increase margins
  • Kalyan's higher GML/sales ratio = slightly more exposed

Working Capital

  • Inventory days: Titan ~130 | Kalyan ~96 (better turnover)
  • Titan hedges ~30–40% of gold risk via MCX futures
  • GHS prepayments = net WC benefit for Titan

⚡ Gold Price Correction
Gold at ₹94,000/10g — historically elevated. A 15–20% correction triggers demand pause, inventory loss, both stocks could correct 20–30%.
⚡ Import Duty Reversal
Budget 2024 cut duty from 15% → 6%. Any reversal would significantly impact volumes across the sector.
⚡ Titan Valuation Risk
At 85x P/E, any minor earnings miss triggers sharp correction. FY27 EPS growth needed: 18–20%.
⚠ Lab-Grown Diamond Disruption
LGD prices down 70% in 4 years. Risk to Titan's high-margin studded jewellery mix over FY27–29.
⚠ Kalyan Franchise Quality Control
Rapid FOCO expansion risks brand integrity. One major incident could damage regional trust built over decades.
⚠ Kalyan Promoter Concentration
Family holds ~62.9%. Limited independent oversight. High promoter concentration warrants governance monitoring.
